Three-minute Training

【21】Prom is a special dance at the end of the school year. Many high school students go to Prom. It is a big event, and people feel excited and happy. At Prom, students celebrate their school year and their friendships. They dress up in nice clothes. Many boys wear suits, and many girls wear dresses. Some students do their hair and make-up. They take photos with friends and with a partner or date. 　Prom is often in a large hall or hotel. There is music, lights, and a DJ. Students dance with friends and dance with a partner. They laugh, talk, and enjoy the night. Some schools give small awards, like “Best Smile.” There is often a special song, and many students sing together. 　Before Prom, students make a plan. Some ask a friend to be a partner. Some invite a date. They plan how to go: by bus, by car, or with parents. They plan what to wear and when to meet. After Prom, students go home safely. Teachers also come to help and watch. 　Prom is not only a party. It is a time to say goodbye to the school year and to look forward to the future. Many students keep Prom memories for a long time. It is a sweet and joyful night.
